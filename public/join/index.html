Get started in 3 steps.

1. Make sure you're ready to dive in.
We will do everything we can to welcome you and make this a meaningful, powerful experience, but we need your help. This isn't your standard online course, it's not for everyone, and getting acquainted with how it works will help ease your transition.

2. Choose the tuition amount that is right for you.
You'll be charged the amount you select once a week for six weeks total (at which point payments will automatically stop). If you want guidance, here's our suggestion: choose a value that feels like a commitment, but also amounts to a total you can comfortably afford.

3. Create an account and start participating.
The same account (email address + password) will be used to access the Course and the Community. Your full name and email will be securely protected and not shared within the community.
