Get Help

If you don't find your issue below, please email us at team@sjmd.space and ask for whatever help you need. We'll do our best to respond within a day.

How do I access my account?

To change your password, billing info, update your membership (or cancel it), or do other account-related things, head to sjmd.memberful.com.

The course or community is displaying weird.

Are you using a modern browser? We recommend Firefox (great privacy settings + not spying on you = win), but any modern browser (e.g., Safari, Edge, Chrome, Brave) that has been updated recently should work. Check your browser settings for an update, and make sure Javascript is enabled.

I'm not receiving the emails with new Modules and course links.

You should get an email every seven days after you sign up for the course. If you're not, one of two things are likely happening here. First, double-check that you signed up with the correct email address (no typos, etc.). If that's not it, odds are the emails are being caught in your spam folder. Seach your spam folder for "Social Justice, Minus Dogma" (here's how for Gmail users) and you should find the emails. If you've done both of these steps, email us and we'll get to the bottom of it.

How do I cancel my membership?

Log in to your account and toggle auto-renew off from the Subscriptions tab: sjmd.memberful.com/account/subscriptions You can cancel at any point, for any reason. You'll lose access to the community, but will be able to continue accessing the course.
